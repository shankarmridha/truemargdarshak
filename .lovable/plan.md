

# True Margdarshak Website Plan

## Overview
A minimal & elegant single-page website targeting C-Suite executives, designed to establish credibility and drive consultations for your corporate training, coaching, and keynote speaking services.

---

## Website Sections (Scrollable Single Page)

### 1. **Hero Section**
- Powerful headline communicating your value proposition to executives
- Subheadline highlighting transformation in Leadership, AI Adoption, and Trainer Development
- Two prominent CTAs: "Book a Discovery Call" and "Request a Consultation"
- Subtle, professional background imagery or abstract visuals

### 2. **About True Margdarshak**
- Your story, mission, and unique approach to corporate transformation
- Key differentiators that resonate with C-Suite decision makers
- Trust indicators (years of experience, companies served, etc.)

### 3. **5 Intelligence Framework**
Five intelligences with elegant cards:
- **Human Intelligence** - IQ which helps in logical decision making
- **Emotional Intelligence** - Ability to connect and maintain the human relations
- **Artificial Intelligence** - Leveraging the AI for higher efficiency
- **Local Intelligence** - Ability to understand and connect with the local ecosystem
- **Spiritual Intelligence** - Being spiritually aware with alignment with the higher self


### 4. **Program Spotlight: Focus Areas**
Detailed sections for your three key areas:
- **Leadership Development** - Building future-ready leaders
- **AI Adoption & Mindset** - Helping organizations embrace AI transformation
- **Train the Trainer** - Empowering internal training capabilities

### 5. **Technical Training**
Enterprise implementation support:
- SAP implementations
- Cyber Security
- Other technical training offerings
- Positioned as complementary to transformation programs

### 6. **Client Success Stories**
- Featured testimonials from executives and organizations
- Case study highlights with measurable outcomes
- Logo showcase of notable clients (if available)

### 7. **Insights Hub**
A resource section featuring:
- **Video Gallery** - Embedded keynote clips, training previews, thought leadership videos
- **Downloadable Resources** - Whitepapers and PDFs with email capture for lead generation
- **LinkedIn Feed Integration** - Display your latest LinkedIn posts/articles to showcase ongoing thought leadership

### 8. **Contact & Booking**
- **Inquiry Form** - Name, email, company, interest area, message
- **Calendar Booking** - Embedded scheduling tool (Calendly/Cal.com integration) for discovery calls
- Direct contact information (email, phone)
- Office location (if applicable)

---

## Design Approach

### Visual Style: Minimal & Elegant
- Clean white space with sophisticated typography
- Muted, professional color palette (perhaps deep navy, charcoal, with a refined accent color)
- Subtle animations on scroll for engagement
- High-quality imagery conveying executive presence and transformation

### Executive-Focused UX
- Clear, scannable content structure
- Prominent CTAs without being aggressive
- Mobile-responsive for executives on the go
- Fast loading and professional polish

---

## Backend & Integrations

### Lead Management
- Contact form submissions stored in a database
- Email notifications when new inquiries arrive
- Downloadable resources with email capture (gated content)

### Calendar Integration
- Embedded booking widget for discovery calls
- Integration with Calendly, Cal.com, or similar

### Content Management
- Resource uploads (PDFs, videos)
- LinkedIn content display via embed or API

---

## Key Features Summary

| Feature | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Hero with dual CTAs | Drive immediate action |
| Services showcase | Communicate offerings clearly |
| Focus area deep-dives | Demonstrate expertise in key areas |
| Video gallery | Build credibility through visual content |
| Gated resources | Lead generation through valuable content |
| LinkedIn integration | Showcase ongoing thought leadership |
| Testimonials & case studies | Social proof for C-Suite buyers |
| Contact form + calendar | Multiple paths to engagement |

---

This website will position True Margdarshak as a premium, trusted partner for corporate transformation while making it easy for busy executives to learn about your services and book consultations.
